Answering common questions about outstanding scholarship program

outstanding scholarship program
Questions Answer
Where can I apply for the scholarship? There are officials at the university who monitor the students GPA, and your name will appear to them if you are an outstanding student. Then you will be contacted via university email.
How much will be deducted from the installments? if the GPA= 4.0 the 40% is deducted
if the GPA = 3.50 the 28% is deducted
if the GPA = 3.0 the 0% is deducted
If the students stumble a little with the GPA, will the scholarship be withdrawn from them? You will not be held accountable if you stumble twice(onlu twice)
What are the types of scholarships? Full scholarship, Partial scholarship


Answering common questions about Special cases fund program

Special cases fund program
Questions Answer
What are the special cases that are accepted for the scholarship? Any condition related to the financial condition (provided that the student is outstanding)
Any physical disability (such as: blindness, body paralysis, refugees, etc.)
What is the discount percentage? It depends on the student's grade point average and excellence
Where can I apply for this scholarship? You can come personally to the university and go to Student Affairs/financial affairs and they will handle all transactions for you.
Or you can contact the financial affairs via email.
